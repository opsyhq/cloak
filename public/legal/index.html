Privacy

Cloak is designed so that we cannot read your secrets.

  • Secrets are encrypted in your browser before they reach our server. The encryption key is part of the URL fragment and never sent to us.
  • We store only the encrypted ciphertext and a server-generated ID. We cannot decrypt your data.
  • Secrets are permanently deleted after one read or when the TTL expires.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
  • No personal information is collected or stored.
